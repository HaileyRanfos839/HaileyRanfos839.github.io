LOCAL GROWN LOVE

About Us

Our company was founded in 2023 after we noticed that many people in our community did not have access to healthy, sustainably grown fruits and vegetables. We pride ourselves on being a family owned business. When we started Local Grown Love, we didn't even know it. All of this started with us giving away food to neighbors and friends, then showing them how to grow their own food. The turning point was when we started hosting a booth at out local farmer's market. We were blown away by the support that we received. This was when we knew that our passion growing our own food could really make an impact.

Why Use Us?

What distinguishes our food from what you could buy at the grocery store? Our source being our backyard guarentees that our food is fresh and well maintained. Our daily checks and short travel provides you the highest quality, which is exactly what you and your loved ones deserve. Our fresh food always you to purchase fruits and vegetables that are at peak freshness, providing optimal nutrition and flavor. Buying through us also makes an impact. For every 10 pounds of produce that we sell, we donate 1 pound to local organizations that are fighting hunger. Find out for yourself why so many in our community have made the switch. Contact Us!
