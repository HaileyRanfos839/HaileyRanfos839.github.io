Our Policies

Financing Policies

We want to be able to work with the community to find a solution for everyone to be able to enjoy farm fresh produce! We accept: Cash, Check, Credit Card, and Venmo. Please be aware that a 3% program fee will be added to all credit card purchases. Unique to small businesses, we are willing to work with what you have to barter! On a case by case basis, we are willing to discuss payments outside of currency such as other goods and services.

Available Discounts

  • Senior Discount: 10%
    • Ages 55+ with a valid ID
  • Veterans Discount: 15%
    • Valid with a military ID
  • Student Discount: 10%
    • Valid with a student ID

Delivery Policies

Local deliveries are available are typically fullfilled within 2-3 days of your purchase. Local shipping qualifies as being within a 15 miles. For deliveries that must be shipped out, shipping fees may and apply. Delivery times change based on location and can be estimated when the purchase is made.

Return Policy

Due to our products being perishable, we do not accept any returns 5 days after purchase and anything before that will be on a case by case basis. We will accept anything that was spoiled within 1 day of purchase for a full refund.
